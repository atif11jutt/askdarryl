I want to find a manufacturer in China for my product but I don't know where to start.
Here's your step-by-step starting point:

1. Go to Alibaba.com and search your product category. Filter for "Verified Supplier" and "Trade Assurance" only.
2. Contact at least 10 factories — not 2 or 3. Get competing quotes.
3. Before sharing ANY details, have them sign an NDA first.
4. Request a sample order — never pay tooling ($2k–$10k) without a signed sample agreement.

What is your product? I'll give you more specific advice.
It's a toy for kids, physical activity focused.
Perfect category — that's exactly my space. For physical toys, look for factories in Guangdong and Zhejiang provinces. Your tooling will likely be $3k–$6k. I went through this exact process for TeeterToss. Want me to walk you through the Alibaba RFQ process step by step?
